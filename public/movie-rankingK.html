Movie Rankings According to Kinga

My TOP 3 movies of all-time.

Suzume

It is a recently watched movie.
It is an anime about about a young high school girl, who by change is met with a mysterious traveler.
Later she is faced with the reality that across Japan there are abandoned doors, which need to be locked and sealed so a so-called worm monster won't release its wrath upon the given city, causing massive earthquakes.
She can also lock doors so together they embark on a journey to seal every single one of them.

Oppenheimer

A very intriguing movie, loved to learn about the "Father of the Atomic Bomb".
Also it has one of the best soundtracks ever. Cillian Murphy is one of my favorite actors for sure.

Bullet Train

It's hard to tell what is this about but I can assure you its going to make you laugh.
It was a very funny and relaxing film to watch. Absolutely love when Brad Pitt has silly characters to play.
